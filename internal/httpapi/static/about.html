Whither

Redirect a Wikipedia article name to that entity's website.

Usage

https://whither.link/{Article_Name}

Example: https://whither.link/Anna's_Archive

Sources

URLs sourced from Wikidata (property P856) licensed under CC0 1.0. Article metadata is sourced from Wikipedia, licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

Code available at @whither-link/whither.
